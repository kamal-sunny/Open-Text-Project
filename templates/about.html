How to Play

WordQuest 5 is a daily word-guess challenge designed for quick, addictive fun. Each round picks a secret five-letter English word from our curated list. Your mission is to reveal it in as few guesses as possible.

Every guess gives feedback with vibrant color codes, helping you zero in on the hidden word. You can attempt up to three games per day, so use your guesses wisely!

Each day, you can attempt up to 3 secret words. For each word you have 5 guesses.

  • Correct letter, wrong position
  • Letter not in the word
